Privacy Policy

LAST UPDATED: MAY 2026 · thegreatreplacement.fyi

/ 01

What we collect

When you use the calculator we collect your job title and state to compute your score. If you purchase a report, we also collect your email address to deliver it. Payment details are processed directly by Stripe — we never see or store your card number.

/ 02

How we use it

Your email is used solely to deliver the PDF report you purchased. We do not send marketing email, newsletters, or any unsolicited correspondence. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third party for their own use.

/ 03

Third-party services

We use Stripe for payment processing (stripe.com/privacy) and Resend for transactional email delivery (resend.com/privacy). Both services receive only the minimum data needed to complete your transaction.

/ 04

Cookies & analytics

We use Plausible Analytics (plausible.io), a privacy-respecting analytics service that uses no cookies and does not track individuals across sites. No personal data leaves your browser. We use no advertising cookies or third-party tracking pixels.

/ 05

Data retention

We retain your email address and report data for up to 90 days in case you need a re-delivery. You can request deletion at any time by emailing us.

/ 06

Contact

Questions or deletion requests: support@thegreatreplacement.fyi
